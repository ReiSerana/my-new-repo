Bibliotheek Hilversum (Central)

  • Address & Contact: ‘’s-Gravelandseweg 55, 1217 EH Hilversum’’ (Central Library)
  • Description / Special Facilities:In addition to lending books, this library hosts a wide variety of children’s and family-friendly events including reading parties, Medialab activities (makerspace, 3D printing), and themed workshops. They also run a “Voorlezen aan peuters (2–4 years)” session every Wednesday from 10:30 to 11:00 at the Central Library — a free, open in-loop activity for toddlers and their parents. Another recurring program is “Voorlezen aan kinderen 4–7 jaar” on Wednesdays at 15:00 to 15:30. They also run “Engels Voorlezen” for children 2–6 years on Wednesday mornings — reading English picture books, singing songs, introducing new words. No registration required. From time to time, the library organizes special events — for example, during Media Ukkie Dagen, children (2–6 years) and their caregivers can explore different media forms like interactive digital stories or small robots in library locations including the Central branch and Servicepunt Noord.
  • Opening Hours & Program Schedule: Regular children’s reading programs (toddlers, preschoolers) happen weekly, typically mid-week. Special events are announced ahead of time (check the library’s event calendar) Some workshops, like a mini-techlab for children aged 4–8, are held at Servicepunt Noord.
  • Many of the children’s reading events (voorlezen) are free and do not require registration. Special events or workshops may charge a small fee or ask for registration — check the event listing.

Bibliotheek Hilversum – Servicepunt Noord

  • Address & Contact: Kamerlingh Onnesweg 245, 1223 KJ Hilversum
  • Description / Special Facilities: Bibliotheek Hilversum – Servicepunt Noord is a smaller, family-friendly library branch designed for easy community access. It offers a cozy reading area for children and hosts several local programs aimed at early learning and family engagement. Parents can find a calm and welcoming environment where children can read, play, and participate in creative activities. The library regularly organizes storytelling sessions for young children, creative craft afternoons, and small-scale workshops that introduce kids to technology and media. These sessions are designed to encourage curiosity and learning in a relaxed, hands-on way. Servicepunt Noord is also known for its Mini Techlab sessions, where children aged 4–8 can explore simple technology projects such as coding basics, building with LEGO WeDo, or creating small digital artworks. These activities are supervised by friendly staff and often align with seasonal or educational themes.
  • Opening Hours & Program Schedule: Programs at Servicepunt Noord typically take place during the week, often in the afternoons, to fit school schedules. The library follows the same general opening hours as the central location, but some activities may vary depending on the season or holidays. Storytime sessions and reading programs are usually held mid-week, while creative and digital workshops are announced in advance through the library’s event calendar. Families are encouraged to check the schedule regularly for new programs or one-off special activities like holiday crafts or themed reading events.
  • Registration & Cost: Most children’s activities, such as storytime and reading sessions, are free of charge and open for drop-in participation. Some workshops or tech-related events may require registration due to limited space or materials. Fees, when applicable, are kept low to ensure accessibility for all families.
  • Parents can contact the library directly to sign up for workshops, or simply visit during open sessions. The staff is always available to help families find suitable programs for their children’s ages and interests.

Bibliotheek Naarden-Bussum (Bibliotheek Gooi+)

  • Address & Contact: Wilhelminaplantsoen 18, 1404 JB Bussum Phone: +31 35 697 3000 · Website: Bibliotheek Gooi+ (Naarden/Bussum pages).
  • Description / Special Facilities:Bibliotheek Naarden-Bussum is a community-oriented public library in the Gooi region that serves families and children with dedicated children’s corners, reading areas, and activity programmes. The library is part of the Bibliotheek Gooi+ network, which runs coordinated youth programming across nearby towns (Bussum, Naarden, Muiden, Laren, Huizen). The location frequently hosts storytelling and age-appropriate reading sessions as well as occasional makerspace or digital activities for children.
  • Opening Hours & Program Schedule: Typical opening hours vary by weekday; for example (example schedule from public listings): Tuesday–Friday daytime with extended hours one weekday evening, and Saturday morning availability. Regular children’s story hours and “voorlezen” sessions are scheduled weekly — e.g., baby/toddler read-alouds and preschool storytimes are held at set weekday mornings or afternoons depending on the branch. Program dates and exact times change seasonally and are published on the Bibliotheek Gooi+ events calendar.
  • Registration & Cost: Most children’s activities such as storytime/voorlezen are free and open to drop-in participants. Some workshops or special events (makerspace sessions, limited-capacity workshops) may require registration through the library’s website and can carry a small fee if materials are provided. Library membership is required to borrow physical items; the network provides membership and borrowing info on its site. Check the branch event listing or contact the library for registration details.
