CompaNanny Hendrik Smitstraat

  • Address: Hendrik Smitstraat 10A, 1222 GL, Hilversum
  • Opening Hours & Fees Open Monday to Friday from 07:30 to 18:30. Hourly rate (2025): approximately €12,66 for full daycare.
  • Age Groups & Programs Offered They accept children aged 0-4 years, with separate groups for babies (0–2) and toddlers/pre-schoolers (2–4) in bright rooms. They emphasize a pedagogical philosophy of play and discovery, adapting activities to children’s developmental stages.
  • Meals, Snacks & Extras They provide a warm lunch daily (healthy and organic) and snacks. Luiers, nap materials, and basic care items are taken care of on site; parents may specify preferences or bring special items.
  • Outdoor & Play Areas The garden is spacious and children frequently visit nearby forest, heath, or playgrounds on walks. Groups enjoy mixed indoor and outdoor time, with safe play equipment and natural surroundings.
  • Staff, Ratios & Qualifications Staff are trained and follow the in-house pedagogical vision. The small scale helps caregivers know children and families well.
  • Registration, Tours & Contact You can request a tour or register with no obligation. Contact: phone 035 685 6706
  • Pros & Considerations Because it’s small and nestled in nature, the feel is warm and familiar. Some trade-offs: distances (depending on where you live), and whether availability fits your schedule.

BLOS Kinderdagverblijf (Banckertlaan)

  • Overview & Location: BLOS has a location in Hilversum Zuid at Banckertlaan 132B.
  • Age Groups & Group Structure: They maintain horizontal groups (0–2 years), vertical groups (0–4 years), and dedicated toddler groups (2–4 years).
  • Programs & Approach: They encourage independence, free exploration, and developmental play. Outdoor time is a regular part. They include a “babytuin” (outdoor sleeping / restful garden) concept.
  • Fees & Inclusions: Hourly rate listed as €12,01 gross. Items such as food, diapers, and basic supplies are included—parents don’t need to bring these by default.
  • Facilities & Extra Features: Large group rooms adapted by age, outdoor play area, indoor sand & water play, reading corners. Close to local playgrounds, forest, and park access.
  • Registration & Visits: You can request a tour or register through their website. They have an LRK number and GGD inspection reports publicly available.

Kinderdagverblijf Toermalijn (Bink)

  • Overview & Location: Address: Lopes Diaslaan 453, 1222 VL, Hilversum
  • Philosophy & Multilingual Environment: Children of Dutch and English backgrounds grow together in a warm, trustful environment. They focus on multicultural and inclusive environment.
  • Registration & Availability: You can apply online via Bink’s customer service (035 683 44 99) or via form. They attempt flexibility in days requested; may negotiate partial or alternate days.
  • Programs & Services: Daycare, toddler care, multilingual play, inclusive group settings.
